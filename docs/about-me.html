About Me

I’m a multimedia creative with a passion for achieving a greater freedom of expression through mindful practice within an ever-broadening skillset.

I’m a believer that everything in life is an informant for every other thing in life. What went well and what didn’t. What concepts overlap and which are unique. Understanding exactly why something works the way it does and then discovering that there’s various partitions of other fields of study--reevaluating and rebranding that same set of knowledge from a different perspective and for a different use case. In this glue that binds us is where I feel I find my muse.

Striving to be the best creator I can be starts with developing an intuition about what I’m doing and how best to get there. The way that I work towards that development is by informing myself through hands-on experience and casting a wide net in the subjects I’m willing to educate myself on. In short: if something intrigues me, I want to learn about every step of the process; not being afraid to cross-reference my existing knowledge in order to make deeper connections with the subject matter, and not being afraid to pay my dues in order to earn, maintain, and progress a working competency of it.

My discovery of loving discovery has lead me to performing on stage with 10 different instruments, repairing musical instruments and electronics, editing hundreds of photos and videos, educating myself from fields such as graphic design and arts management, and my latest venture of learning web development/design; with my first project being the website you’re reading this on right now ;)

TL;DR: The world around us is fascinating; learn about it.
