Summary

Growing up in Southeast Wisconsin, I was heavily involved in my high school music program; performing in the concert band, symphonic wind ensemble, jazz band, and jazz combo, as well as extracurricular ensembles. Eventually I found my way to the University of Wisconsin - Stevens Point, where in my musical studies I was taken under the wing of David Hastings (saxophone) and Mathew Buchman (jazz studies) as well as playing under the baton of Dr. Michael Butler (director of bands). I was involved in their jazz ensembles, wind ensemble, concert band, and jazz combos where I performed on multiple saxophones, clarinets, and flute.

Projects

4th Dimension saxophone quartet

2015 - 2019

Saxophone quartet originally formed by members of the Port Washington High School music program and eventually became an expanded group with the addition of more saxophones, guitar, bass, keyboard, and percussion in various iterations and arrangements.

I had the opportunity to play multiple saxophones, clarinet, and arrange music for the group as well as track its social media presence, create a file management system for hundred of pieces of sheet music, and help coordinate/lead rehearsals.

This band prided itself on its versatility; playing a wide array of styles and venues from street performances to private events at the Milwaukee Art Museum.
