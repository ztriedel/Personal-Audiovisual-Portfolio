PWHS Music Archive

Documented and preserved available footage of the Port Washington High School music program; primarily between the 2012-13 and 2015-16 academic years.

Duties included:

  • video editing
    • color correction, image sharpening, stabilizing handheld footage, light audio sweetening
  • organizing videos into YouTube playlists
  • adding proper descriptions/metadata

Project estimates: 375 videos; 26 hours of edited footage; 8 weeks from start to finish
